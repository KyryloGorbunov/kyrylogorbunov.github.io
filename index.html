8 reasons to start working with us

No cash, payment is automatic
The cost of the trip is paid by the passenger with a linked bank card, i.e. there are no risks associated with the transportation of cash for both the passenger and the driver, you do not need to look for petty money for the change.
Commission 8% + BONUSES
We have a minimum commission and we pay bonuses for each trip, some of our competitors leave bonuses to themselves. We work honestly and the drivers earn more!
High income with guaranteed benefits
We pay wages on the driver's personal bank card! You do not need to issue LLC, PE, pay taxes, file a declaration, pay for accounting services and maintaining a bank account.
Work at a convenient time, no schedules
You just press the button to get on the line to start work, when you decide to finish, click off the line. You can only work in the morning, in the evening, on weekends or holidays!
Comfortable, transparent and easy to connect
We connect remotely and for free, you do not need to come to us! You send us copies of documents, we register you and send you data for connection, after 1-2 days you can start working!
Always a lot of orders
The app shows where the most orders are at the moment and where there are higher fares. You will not have downtime and idle journeys. Our passengers are always pleasant and absolutely non-conflict people who are happy to drive.
24/7 support
Uber offers 24-hour email support for drivers. If something is unclear, you can always ask a question via On-line consultation.
Without checkers and stickers
You do not need to change the color, install «checkers» on the car and stick it with stickers.
